Dhrupad in the Grove

Bahauddin Dagar and Pelva Naik, Saturday July 19th at 5:30pm

An Invitation from the Bangalore Dhrupad Group

On the evening of the 19th of July 2013, Bahauddin Dagar and Pelva Naik will be performing Dhrupad at Grove 4, Good Earth Orchard, Kengeri. The performance will start at 5:30 pm.

As the venue is a small and intimate space that seats only a 100 people, please RSVP here. The contribution per guest is Rs. 500. To help us plan better, we are adopting a first-come-first-served approach. Your attendance will be considered confirmed only after the payment is received.

Please take a moment to read through the profiles of the artistes below.

Warm regards,
The Bangalore Dhrupad Group.

Pelva Naik

Pelva Naik, a young and forthcoming artist of her generation, has been trained in the Dhrupad style of vocal music under the eminent Dagar Gharana of Dhrupad music.

Born in 1986, in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Pelva was brought up in a family of art lovers and connoisseurs; where she was nurtured amid affluent virtues of Indian culture, art and music.

She received her initial training in Dhrupad from Guru Ustad Zia Fariduddin Dagar Saheb and has also received proficient guidence from Shree Bahauddin Dagar. She has been full time involved in the practice and study of the Dhrupad Art.

She teaches Dhrupad at the Rajghatl-Krishnamurti Foundation India, Varanasi and in Bangalore.
